An isometric turn based RPG on a hexagon map that expands as you explore the fog of war surrounding your initial start as a courier in a vault based in the play style and graphics of Civilization 4, using the factions and story points of Fallout: New Vegas.

When you go inside a building, a vault, a cave, or a special location like Hoover Dam, The Strip, or Caesar’s Fort, it loads a new map for you to explore, giving more depth and detail to each world with different graphics and themes.

Your character, The Courier is a powerful scout, with dialogue options that determine your standing with the factions, which quests you get given, and which ending you get at the end of the game as you meet NPCs from various factions who give you quests.

Your starting vault grows and develops, but the main progression is of your character as you level up your skills which help you with various challenges.

Three types of quests:
Companion quests, as your dialogue with your various companions you walk with evolves, certain locations trigger them to say certain things.

Faction quests, usually as you Kill or convince an enemy, involving some complications and choices and the right dialogue choices

Retrieve an item from a dangerous location with lots of enemies to help a faction,  companion, or yourself.
